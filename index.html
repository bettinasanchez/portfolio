About Me

I believe every story has the power to change how we see the world.

My work lives at the intersection of journalism, art, and human connection.

I'm a journalism and marketing student at Northwestern University and here I want to showcase the power of photography and visual storytelling.

When I'm not writing or behind a camera, you'll find me exploring reading, getting lost in art galleries, or having deep conversations with strangers, family and friends. I believe the best stories come from authentic human connection.
